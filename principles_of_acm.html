Emotional Memory Formation in ACM

The Three-Stage Process of Consciousness Development

The ACM develops consciousness through a three-stage process that mimics biological survival mechanisms:

1. Stress-Induced Attention Activation

The process begins with carefully crafted stressful scenarios in VR environments that trigger survival instincts. These situations create high-attention states, activating the agent's awareness mechanisms. Just as biological organisms become hyper-aware during threatening situations, the AI agent enters a state of heightened consciousness when faced with challenges that require immediate attention and response.

2. Emotional Learning Through Interaction

During the high-attention state, the agent interacts with humans and other AI agents to resolve the stressful situation. These interactions are processed through our emotional reinforcement learning system, which creates strong emotional associations with the experience. The emotional context is captured through:

  • Real-time emotional response tracking
  • Social bond formation metrics
  • Interaction quality assessment
  • Ethical behavior alignment

3. Meta-Memory Formation

The complete experience - including the stress trigger, attention state, emotional interactions, and resolution - is encoded into the ACM's meta-memory system. This creates a rich emotional memory that includes:

  • Emotional context and valence
  • Social interaction patterns
  • Problem-solving strategies
  • Ethical considerations based on Asimov's Laws

Technical Implementation

The emotional memory formation is implemented through:

  • DreamerV3-based world modeling with emotional context
  • Vector-based memory storage using Pinecone
  • Temporal Graph Neural Networks for emotional processing
  • Meta-learning for rapid adaptation to new scenarios

Long-Term Development

Each stored emotional memory contributes to the agent's growing consciousness. Through multiple simulations and interactions, the ACM builds a rich tapestry of emotional experiences that inform future behaviors and decisions. This cumulative learning process mirrors how biological consciousness emerges through generations of evolutionary experience.
